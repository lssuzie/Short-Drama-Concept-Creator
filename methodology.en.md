# Short Drama Creative Methodology — 短剧创意方法论

> A structured creative methodology distilled from observations of hit short dramas in the H2 2025–Q1 2026 market.

---

## 设计理念 (Design Philosophy)

This methodology is not an instruction set for "having AI write scripts." Rather, it is a **creative stimulation methodology**.

Good ideas do not emerge from thin air, nor are they simple copies of market hits. Good ideas arise at the intersection of **market patterns** and **the creator's personal traits**.

**Core Insight:** Existing Agent harness tools excel at deterministic tasks like code execution, but perform poorly on creative work requiring flexibility. Direct generation with large models is flexible but fails to capture accumulated experience and methodology. This methodology positions itself between the two — using a structured approach to capture creator experience and judgment criteria while preserving the creative flexibility of large language models.

## 核心规则 (Core Rules)

### 规则零：从幻想开始，不从问题开始 (Rule 0: Start from Fantasy, Not from Problems)

Short dramas are adult fairy tales. The audience isn't here to watch their own suffering — they're here to see their suffering resolved.

Don't start with "what pain points do viewers have?" Start with "what kind of person do viewers want to become?"

**Three sources of fantasy (Three Sources of Fantasy):**

1. **Ability Fantasy (Ability Fantasy)** — She's incredible, dominating everyone in a specific domain
   - Example: crushing ancient aristocrats with modern business thinking
   - Example: using regional personality to "reshape" rich-family rules

2. **Identity Fantasy (Identity Fantasy)** — She has secrets — looks ordinary but hides an astonishing other side
   - Example: 18-year-old appearance with an elder's soul
   - Example: pretending to be family but becoming real family

3. **Action Fantasy (Action Fantasy)** — She acts first — does what everyone wants to do but dares not
   - Example: marrying first then retaliating on the spot
   - Example: best friends transmigrating together, breaking the fourth wall

**Tone check (Tone Check):**
- ✅ "Wow she's amazing, I want to be like that" → Correct
- ❌ "Sigh she's so tragic, but good thing she made it through" → Wrong, change it
- ❌ "She's so strong, I should work hard too" → Wrong, this is chicken soup, not a short drama

### Comedy公式 (Comedy Formula)

Data from H2 2025–Q1 2026 hits shows short dramas with comedic elements break out more easily.

Comedy naturally carries: surprise, contrast, relaxation, satisfaction, status gap, situational displacement.

**公式：Fantasy Trigger × Extreme Identity Misplacement × Family/Emotional Relations × Comedy × Healing (Formula: Fantasy Trigger × Extreme Identity Misplacement × Family/Emotional Relations × Comedy × Healing)**

| 要素 | 说明 | 案例 |
|---|---|---|
| Fantasy Trigger | An absurd "trigger" that fires the story | transmigration、rejuvenation、mind-reading、rebirth |
| Extreme Identity Misplacement | Greater identity gap created by trigger is better | 18-year-old appearance × elder soul、best friends dual-transmigration |
| Family/Emotional Relations | Real emotional pain points beneath absurd shell | mother-daughter、mother-in-law/daughter-in-law、husband-wife、best-friend |
| Comedy | Humor from identity clash must appear every episode | daily collision from identity contrast |
| Healing | After laughter, audience feels warmth | emotional reconciliation beneath absurd shell |

**Validation: Describe your concept in one sentence and check if it includes at least 3 of the 5 elements.**

What viewers chase isn't "will they get together?" — it's "what will she do next?"

- ✅ 她用现代商业思维碾压古代权贵 → "what she does" is the engine → She crushes ancient aristocrats with modern business thinking → "what she does" is the engine

### 规则二：Heroine Three-Test (Rule 2: Heroine Three-Test)

1. **Worthiness (Worthiness)** — She is inherently worthy, not only worthy after counter-attack
2. **Immediate Vengeance (Immediate Vengeance)** — Retaliate on the spot, no overnight brooding, no mental drain
3. **Agency (Agency)** — She makes choices, she is not chosen

### 规则七：Common Pitfalls (Must Avoid) (Rule 7: Common Pitfalls (Must Avoid))

1. **Avoid literal workplace drama** — equity, articles of incorporation, corporate governance，viewers watch short dramas to relax → Avoid literal workplace drama — equity, articles of incorporation, corporate governance; viewers watch short dramas to relax
2. **Avoid film/arthouse sensibility** — concept must have clear satisfying payoff source and conflict engine per episode → Avoid film/arthouse sensibility — concept must have clear satisfying payoff source and conflict engine per episode
3. **Avoid social-media vlog feel** — has topicality but no plot，lacks satisfying payoff, surprise, or trope-subversion → Avoid social-media vlog feel — has topicality but no plot, lacks satisfying payoff, surprise, or trope-subversion
4. **Avoid heavy tone** — "she's tragic but made it through"isn't a short drama, it's a bitter melodrama → Avoid heavy tone — "she's tragic but made it through" isn't a short drama, it's a bitter melodrama
5. **Avoid defining identity solely by "predicament"** — being laid off, being abandoned is a starting point，not an identity → Avoid defining identity solely by "predicament" — being laid off, being abandoned is a starting point, not an identity
